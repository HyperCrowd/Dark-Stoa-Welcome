From Season 3 to Season 4

Season 3 was an experiment. Those who watched all of Season 1 and 2 were able to join a Mattermost server if they found the link to it. In this experiment, everyone came from random walks of life and had the ability to ban everyone. There was no way I could prevent that banning. After a year of organic discussion about Dark Stoa-related topics, not a single person used the ban option. The results were obvious: Dark Stoa Seasons 1 and 2 acts as a trust filter.

Season 4 starts with the survivors of Season 3. They will be empowered with tools and techniques to establish overwatch and influence random domains of interest. Each domain will autobalance to the shortcomings of another domain based on a few math equations I've developed.

Your task, humble newbie, is to find a way into those domains. We still start you off with the following channels:

  • Announcements: Events that affect Season 4 will show up here. (read-only)
  • Discussion: The general discussion room to ask questions and interact with the Dark Stoa community.
  • Memes: High-impact, low-grammar gems of context.
  • News: A feed of Dark Stoa topics as they appear on Reddit, Twitter, YouTube, Google Trends, and the news.
  • Season 1: Discussion, questions, and examinations of the concepts discussed in Season 1.
  • Season 2: Discussion, questions, and examinations of the concepts discussed in Season 2.
  • The Off Season: Discussion, questions, and examinations of the concepts discussed in off-season Dark Stoa content.
